One of the most common problems when studying nature is being able to determine the behavior of some variables that depend on another group of variables. In this case we model a set of points of the form (X,Y) where y expresses the desired behavior for a given value of x. In general, the objective is to obtain a function that expresses a property or the behavior of a phenomenon that is defined as form (X,Y).
